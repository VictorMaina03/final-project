About Us

Our Story

PetCare was established in 2024, born out of a genuine love for animals and a desire to make a positive impact on their lives. Our founder, Victor Maina, envisioned a place where pets would receive not only top-notch medical care but also the love and respect they deserve.

Over the months, our journey has been marked by growth, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to the welfare of animals.

Our Services

1.Pet Sitting

Whether you’re away for a day or an extended period, our trusted pet sitters will take care of your furry friends in the comfort of your own home. We offer:

  • Daily visits: Feeding, playtime, and walks.
  • Overnight stays: Ensuring your pet feels safe and loved around the clock.
  • Special needs care: Administering medications and attending to special dietary requirements.

2.Dog Walking

Our professional dog walkers provide your canine companion with the exercise and socialization they need to stay healthy and happy. Options include:

  • Daily walks: Customized to your dog’s energy level and preferences.
  • Group walks: Socialize with other dogs in a safe, supervised environment.
  • Adventure walks: Extended outings to local parks and trails.

3.Grooming

Keep your pet looking and feeling their best with our comprehensive grooming services. We offer:

  • Bathing and brushing: Regular grooming to maintain a healthy coat.
  • Nail trimming: Ensuring your pet’s comfort and preventing overgrowth.
  • Haircuts and styling: Customized grooming for all breeds.

4.Pet Boarding

When you need to be away, our comfortable boarding facilities provide a home away from home for your pet. Features include:

  • Spacious accommodations: Cozy, clean, and safe environment.
  • Daily activities: Playtime, exercise, and social interaction.
  • Personalized care: Attention to dietary and medical needs.

5.Veterinary Services

Our on-site veterinary partners offer a range of medical services to keep your pet in optimal health:

  • Routine check-ups: Regular health assessments and vaccinations.
  • Emergency care: Immediate attention for urgent medical needs.
  • Specialized treatments: Dental care, surgeries, and chronic condition management.
